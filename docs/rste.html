About RSTE

The RSTE (Refrangible Society of Technophile Engineers) is a Non Government organisation (NGO) registered under Act 1973,Section 44 is the elite name for development of EVs, sustainable energy and creative education. Society is mentoring thousands of young engineering students and entrepreneurs with the association of scientists, researchers and various industry experts from all over the globe and providing the best opportunity to meet the criteria of current demand of industry. Solar electric car event (SUVC) ,Workshops, training, industries visit, product development ,Internship and placement are the main key areas of society.

MILESTONE

2017

FOUNDED RSTE SOCIETY

Founded RSTE society on 26 August 2017 with registration and all the legalities. Collaboration with Gwalior Vyapar Mela, SUVC event registration, Pre-virtual round hosted in gwalior, Madhya pradesh.

2018

SUVC Season-1

SUVC Season-1 conducted at Gwalior Madhya pradesh, Training program conducted in Aurangabad and Mumbai. Association with AMV solar, Solar India and Launch India as corporate association. E- bicycle product launch and work on solar research .

2019

CONDUCTED SAP TRAINING PROGRAMME

SAP training program conducted at Bangalore, Gwalior, Trichy, Coimbatore, aurangabad and chennai in various Institutes. Collaboration with Launch India, illuvia Industries, Messer cutting as placement partners. Conducted SUVC -2019 Season 2 in coimbatore, Tamilnadu with association of Hindusthan College of Engineering & Technology (HCET).
